GUARDIÃO TUPÃ

Roles: Creative Director, Lead Developer

Guardião Tupã is a passion project being produced in my spare time, it has support from the São Paulo government through cultural investment. It's a 3D adventure game/platformer where you take on the role of a newly created magical being destined to restore the balance of the world. The game features strong references to Brazilian folklore and fauna and has a demo scheduled for release in 2024.
